Microsoft FY21 Fourth Quarter Earnings Conference Call, July 27, 2021. Amy Hood discussed the FY22 operating plan and the allocation of operating expense. AMY HOOD: The question we ask for every operating expense dollar we invest is, are we continuing to invest it in the highest growth places? If you continue to invest in high growth places with differentiation that customers care about, the overall amount should grow based on the opportunity. Amy Hood also stated that headcount growth would be 12 percent plus and that the opportunity and prior execution supported confidence in that investment level.

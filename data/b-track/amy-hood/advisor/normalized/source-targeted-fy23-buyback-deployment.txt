Microsoft Corporation Form 10-K for the fiscal year ended June 30, 2023, filed July 27, 2023. The filing reports actual deployment under the company's authorized share repurchase programs. During fiscal years 2023 and 2022, we repurchased 69 million shares and 95 million shares of our common stock for $18.4 billion and $28.0 billion, respectively, through our share repurchase programs. The authorization remained available, while the amount deployed in fiscal 2023 was materially lower than in fiscal 2022. This record establishes the observed allocation change but does not by itself disclose management's complete causal explanation.

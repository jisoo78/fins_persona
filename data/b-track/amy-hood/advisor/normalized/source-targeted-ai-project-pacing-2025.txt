Noelle Walsh, President of Microsoft Cloud Operations and Innovation, explained changes to the 2025 datacenter build plan. She wrote that planning is multi-year and capital intensive and requires agility as Microsoft learns with customers. NOELLE WALSH: What this means is that we are slowing or pausing some early-stage projects. She added that Microsoft would strategically pace plans while continuing to grow and align investments with business priorities and customer demand. This executive statement confirms a selective timing and location adjustment rather than abandonment of the broader AI infrastructure strategy.

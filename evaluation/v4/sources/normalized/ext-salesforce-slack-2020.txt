Salesforce Signs Definitive Agreement to Acquire Slack
Published: 2020-12-01

Salesforce and Slack entered into a definitive agreement under which Salesforce would acquire Slack. Slack shareholders would receive $26.79 in cash and 0.0776 shares of Salesforce common stock per share, representing an enterprise value of approximately $27.7 billion. Salesforce said the combination would create an operating system for the new way to work. The boards of both companies approved the transaction. Salesforce expected to fund the cash portion with new debt and cash on its balance sheet.

Intel Reports Second-Quarter 2024 Results and Announces Cost Reduction Plan
Published: 2024-08-01

Intel announced a $10 billion cost-reduction plan intended to increase efficiency and market competitiveness. The plan included reducing gross capital expenditures by more than 20 percent from prior projections and taking proactive spending reductions to improve profits and strengthen the balance sheet. Chief Financial Officer David Zinsner tied the action to gross-margin pressure, unused capacity, and the need to keep executing the company's transformation.

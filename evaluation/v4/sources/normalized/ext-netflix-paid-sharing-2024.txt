Netflix Fourth Quarter 2023 Earnings Call
Published: 2024-01-23

Chief Financial Officer Spencer Neumann said that Netflix's 2023 pricing and plans focus was rolling out paid sharing and that it made almost no price increases until late in the year. Looking ahead, the company kept its pricing philosophy: continually improve the service, create more member value, and occasionally price into that value, while managing overall revenue growth rather than a single average-revenue target.

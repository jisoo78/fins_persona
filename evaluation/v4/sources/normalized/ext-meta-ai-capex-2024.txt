Meta Reports First Quarter 2024 Results
Published: 2024-04-24

Meta reported first-quarter capital expenditures of $6.72 billion. The company raised its full-year 2024 capital expenditure outlook to $35 billion to $40 billion from $30 billion to $37 billion as it accelerated infrastructure investments to support its artificial-intelligence roadmap. It also expected capital expenditures to continue increasing in 2025 while investing aggressively in AI research and product development.

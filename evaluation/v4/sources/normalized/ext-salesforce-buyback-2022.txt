Salesforce Announces Strong Second Quarter Fiscal 2023 Results
Published: 2022-08-24

Salesforce initiated its first share-repurchase program, authorizing up to $10 billion. Chief Financial Officer Amy Weaver said the company was delivering disciplined, profitable growth at scale and had a capital-allocation strategy intended to improve its long-term position. The authorization added a shareholder-return tool while preserving discretion over timing and actual deployment.

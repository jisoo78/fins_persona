Salesforce Signs Definitive Agreement to Acquire Slack
Published: 2020-12-01

Salesforce and Slack entered a definitive agreement for a mixed cash-and-stock acquisition valued at approximately $27.7 billion. Salesforce described the target as a common operating interface for a new way of working. The boards approved the transaction, and Salesforce expected to fund the cash portion using both balance-sheet cash and new debt.

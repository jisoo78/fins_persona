Costco Q4 2023 Attributable CFO Earnings-Call Transcript
Published: 2023-09-26

Chief Financial Officer Richard Galanti described a future membership-fee increase as a matter of timing rather than inevitability alone. He said inflation and economic pressure mattered, and that strong loyalty gave the company room to wait rather than burden members immediately. This attributable secondary transcript predates the 2024 official increase announcement and supplies the decision-time customer-value rationale; the later official announcement remains the primary event record.
